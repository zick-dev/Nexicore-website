NexiCore Roadmap

Q2 2023

Started development of the CodeNest mobile application.

Q3 2023

Completed the beta testing of CodeNest and gathered user feedback.

Q4 2023

Official launch of CodeNest with offline educational resources.

Q1 2024

Planning for new projects focused on e-commerce solutions and content libraries.

Q2 2024 and Beyond

Expand our offerings to include more educational apps and resources.

Our long-term goal is to empower individuals through accessible offline education, continually evolving our platform to meet user needs.
